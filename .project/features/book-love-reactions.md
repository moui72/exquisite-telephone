On the reveal page players can heart a finished book (positive-only, no negative counterpart), and when a hearted book opened with a player-written free-form prompt, that phrase is logged as a candidate for the curated bank.
Why: surfaces prompts that demonstrably produced a good chain, mining the free-form mode for curated-bank candidates rather than authoring them cold. Positive-only by design so the reveal stays celebratory and no player's book gets publicly downvoted. Shares the persistence gap with [[player-prompt-rating]]: candidate logging must outlive the in-memory room.

Subsumed 2026-07-20 by [[player-prompt-rating]]. Rating the opening
phrase on the drawing turn reaches the same goal — mining good
player-written phrases as bank candidates — without a second reaction
mechanism or any Reveal-page surface: a thumbs-up on a player-written
opening phrase adds it to the candidate pool directly. The heart
reaction itself was dropped rather than deferred. If hearts are wanted
later as a purely social feature, that is a fresh backlog entry, not a
