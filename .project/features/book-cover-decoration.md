Players can decorate their book's cover on a drawing canvas pre-stamped "<username>'s book" while waiting for others: a 30s grace period to keep decorating when their next turn arrives, and a 2-minute end-of-game window (skippable by submitting early, and gating reveal until it closes or all players submit).
Why: turns otherwise-idle wait time into play, and gives every book a personalized cover for the reveal.
