Players and hosts get in-context explanations of how the game works: a dedicated rules-overview panel, explanatory text on the write/draw turn interface, and tooltips or info links for every host-configurable setting (turn timer, laps per book, monochrome toggle, minimum-player override), plus any other spots that turn out to need guiding text.
Why: the game currently has no onboarding or explanation surface at all — new players/hosts have to infer rules and settings meaning from the UI alone.
