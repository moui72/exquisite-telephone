Add a game-creation setting for how many laps each book takes — i.e. how many full rotations through all players a book cycles before the game ends and Reveal begins — configurable by the host at room-creation/lobby time, defaulting to 2 laps when fewer than 5 players and 1 lap otherwise, with a maximum of 3 laps.
Why: with small groups, a single lap (one turn per player) makes for a short game; extra laps let the same small group play longer without needing more people, while larger groups already get enough content from a single lap.
