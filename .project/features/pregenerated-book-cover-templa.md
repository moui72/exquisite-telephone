Players choose from a set of pregenerated geometric/abstract background templates (Fan Deco, Damask Lattice, Marbled Endpaper, Star Chart, Herringbone Cloth, Halftone Bloom, Contour Field, Pennant Row, Houndstooth) as a starting point for their book cover decoration, instead of a blank canvas.
Why: gives players a nicer-looking default and something to draw on top of; light enough opacity/value that ink stays legible. Depends on book-cover-decoration. Design mockup: https://claude.ai/code/artifact/99319838-ae3f-4c2d-b82a-863ef04e92f9
